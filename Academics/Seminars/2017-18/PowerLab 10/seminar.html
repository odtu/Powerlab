Furkan Karakaya received the B.Sc degree in electrical and electronics engineering from Middle East Technical University (METU), Ankara, Turkey, in 2017. He started his M.Sc. in the same department in September 2017. He was employed as a part-time R/D engineer in ASELSAN Inc. during his B.Sc. between January 2017- June 2017. He is currently working as a researcher in METU EE since September 2017 working in Integrated Modular Motor Drive project supported by TUBITAK.
PowerLab organizes regular seminars specifically for students who are planning to choose the Power area(i.e. power electronics-electric machines or power systems), and for students already studying in these areas. However, all students from all departments are very welcome to attend the seminars. For more information please visit PowerLab webpage.
