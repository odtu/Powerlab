Doğa Ceylan received the B.Sc degree in electrical and electronics engineering from Middle East Technical University (METU), Ankara,Turkey, in 2016. He started his M.Sc. in the same department in February 2016. He was employed as a part time R/D engineer in BAMA Technology Inc. during his B.Sc. between July 2015-February 2016 working on rehabilitation robotics. He is currently working as a researcher in METU EE since February 2016 working on electromagnetic finite element analysis and optimization of an electromagnetic launcher funded by ASELSAN Inc.
